Founder and CEO

MockOut (2016 - present)

MockOut is a mobile fantasy game for offseason events such as the NFL and NBA Drafts.

I created Mockout with my partner in May of 2016, despite neither of us having experience in business or app development. By the NFL Draft in April of 2017, we had a basic NFL Draft game and had acquired almost 5,000 users.

Today, MockOut has games for the NFL and NBA Drafts, as well as NFL and NBA free agency, and has over 47,000 users.

To get the app and fall in love with the offseason, go to mockout.com.
